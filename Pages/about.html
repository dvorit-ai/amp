a medium
platform

this website acts as a catalog for the exhibition a medium platform, which is a fluid exhibit made to build community among a large group of designers in a shared space. the more that is contributed, the more life the exhibit has. as weeks go on, the exhibit evolves depending on who and what is being displayed on a given day, and exists as its own living, breathing entity. on this site, you can visit and view what was contributed to the shelf each week.

This project is part of the undergrad thesis exploration by Deborah Khodanovich.

feb.14

midterm presentation

my midterm was focused around my formula for exhibiting ghosts. you take the ghost discipline (i.e. books), create an empty frame or vessel to display it (i.e. the shelf), and then create a frame to document the exhibit (i.e. this website). this video demonstrates exactly this, by physically framing the shelf with the website using projection mapping. visit the feb.14 link to see the masked div.
