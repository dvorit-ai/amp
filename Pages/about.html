a medium
platform

a medium platform is a fluid exhibition created to build community among a large group of designers in a shared space. the more we all contribute, the more life the exhibit will have. as weeks go on, the exhibit evolves depending on who and what is being displayed on a given day, and exists as its own living, breathing entity.
